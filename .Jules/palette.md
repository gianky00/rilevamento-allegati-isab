## 2024-05-22 - [Keyboard Nav for Scanning Workflows]
**Learning:** In "high-volume scanning" interfaces (like ROI selection in PDFs), relying solely on mouse clicks for navigation creates significant friction. Users mentally switch modes between "navigating" and "drawing/scanning".
**Action:** Always map "Previous/Next" actions to Left/Right arrow keys in image/document viewers to allow for rapid, fluid scanning.
